# Abstract MEP terminal products

Source: [`mep-terminal-products.skos.ttl`](sources/mep-terminal-product.ttl)

## Scheme

- **definition (de):** Produkttyp-Klassifikation fuer TGA-Endgeraete und Terminals in versorgten Raeumen (entspricht SystemType.terminal). Fuer Katalog-, Spezifikations- und Kostenworkflows auf Equipment.
- **definition (en):** Product-type classification for MEP end devices and terminals located in served spaces (aligns with SystemType.terminal). For catalog, specification, and cost workflows on Equipment.
- **prefLabel (de):** Abstrakte TGA-Endgeraeteprodukte
- **prefLabel (en):** Abstract MEP terminal products
- **title (en):** Abstract MEP terminal products
