# Abstract facade covering products

Source: [`facade-covering-products.skos.ttl`](sources/facade-covering-product.ttl)

## Scheme

- **definition (de):** Produkttyp-Klassifikation fuer aeussere Fassadenbekleidungen und vorgehaengte Fassadensysteme. Fuer Katalog-, Spezifikations- und Kostenworkflows; mit abstrakter Materialklassifikation fuer dominante Substanz kombinieren.
- **definition (en):** Product-type classification for exterior facade claddings and ventilated facade finish systems. For catalog, specification, and cost workflows; pair with abstract material classification for dominant substance.
- **prefLabel (de):** Abstrakte Fassadenbekleidungsprodukte
- **prefLabel (en):** Abstract facade covering products
- **title (en):** Abstract facade covering products
